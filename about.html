Who We Are

Hampi Cab & Taxi Service Muttu (Tuk Tuk) has been providing reliable taxi services in Hampi for more than 15 years.

We offer comfortable and affordable travel for tourists who want to explore the historical beauty of Hampi and the Vijayanagara Empire.

Our experienced drivers know every historical place and guide visitors to the best sightseeing spots across the Hampi region.

Come and feel the taste of the great Vijayanagar Empire with our friendly and trusted taxi service.
